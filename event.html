Young Ministers Network Disciples

Young Ministers Network Disciples is a plartform that provides the opportunity for personal training and mentorship by Olakunle Davies. It is a class for young people between the ages of 15 and 25 who have a deep hunger for God and to discover His purpose for them and how they can effectively represent Him in the field that He has sent them to. The sessions are infused with anointed, practical and capacity building teaching from Olakunle Davies.
