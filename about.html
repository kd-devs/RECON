Our Mission

The Rebrand Conference is dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of the gospel and promoting authentic worship. We gather believers from around the world to experience powerful worship, insightful sermons, and meaningful fellowship.

Our goal is to equip attendees with the knowledge and inspiration to live out their faith vibrantly in their communities.

We have a deep passion to expose young people especially in their twenties to the tangible power of God. To the end that what was handed unto the generation of The Fathers of faith will not just end with them but also be passed to us.

Our motto is Souls Saved! Bodies Healed! Jesus Glorified!

Hallelujah!!!

Our Values

Faith: We believe in the power of unwavering faith in God and His teachings. Our conference is dedicated to strengthening the faith of all attendees through worship, prayer, and the sharing of personal testimonies.

Community: We value the sense of community and fellowship that comes from gathering together in the name of Christ. Our conference aims to build strong, supportive relationships among believers from diverse backgrounds.

Service: We are committed to serving others as an expression of our faith. Our conference encourages attendees to engage in acts of kindness and service, reflecting the love of Christ in their daily lives.

Discipleship: We prioritize the growth and development of disciples of Christ. Our conference provides resources and teachings that help individuals deepen their understanding of the Gospel and live out their faith more fully.

Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity and transparency in all our actions. Our conference promotes honesty, accountability, and ethical behavior in accordance with Biblical principles.
